Help

Please view the documentation on GitLab for information on how to connect the Database to server.

Use the buttons at the top to increase/decrease the speed for dragging the graph. Dragging can be done by holding left click and moving mouse left or right

You can use the mouse wheel (or two-finger scroll on touchpad) to zoom in and out of the graph. You can also, to zoom into a specific time, type the time (without micro-seconds, just number) into the boxes at the top of the screen to zoom.
